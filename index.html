What is Flitti?

10 Things You Need To Know About Your New Partner In Life

Flitti motivates you to be the BEST person you can possibly be by turning your life into a game.

We do this using Flitti's 7 pillars of life. Sleep, Food, Physical Activity, Work, Education, Passion, and Flex time.

You tell us which of these you excel at and what needs work. Based on where you stand with Flitti’s 7 pillars of life, we suggest teammates for you to connect with that can help you along the way.

You can have up to 49 teammates. These people are preferably strong where you’re weak and vice versa. This allows for a support network for your goals.

Flitti starts you off slow by having you pick one pillar to work on. You can either build upon a strength or address a weak point.

Each of Flitti’s pillars is designed to help you achieve excellence in that area of life.

We realize that there isn’t a one size fits all solution for excellence. That’s why Flitti learns with you and adapts the game of life to give you the best chance for success.

You can look back at the experience you’ve gained in each pillar, or as a whole, and track your growth over time.

Compare your growth against others on Flitti to see how well you’re doing in each pillar.

We’re only getting started. As time progresses we’ll be improving Flitti to help you be the best person you can possibly be.
