What is Flitti? 10 Things You Need to Know about the Newest Social Network

Warning: The information on this page might blow your mind.

Flitti gamifies your life by rewarding the positive things that you do every day. You can collect points for actions that you complete.

You can use your points in the Flitti Store in exchange for items or services posted by other Flitti users.

You can sell anything on the store really. Phones, laptops, other electronics. Anything legal can be sold.

You can earn for things like fitness, online learning, community service, and environmental responsibility. You can buy 1 year expansion packs that let you earn points on sites like Facebook and Twitter.

You can have up to 49 teammates or “friends” on the site. If you complete challenges with teammates you both can earn extra.

If you want to hit it big you can bet some of your points on a coin flip to double them.

You can look back on your earning history and track your social impact.

A bunch of people all doing little things that make the world a better place adds up quickly.

We’re going to be constantly expanding what you can do on the site.

If you sign up now you can start earning right away.
